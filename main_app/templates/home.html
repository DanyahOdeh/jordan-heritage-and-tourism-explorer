Discover the marine places in Jordan

Jordan boasts stunning beaches on the Red Sea and the Dead Sea, making it an ideal destination for marine tourism. These areas offer a variety of activities such as snorkeling, diving, and swimming, in addition to relaxing on picturesque beaches and enjoying unique natural scenery.

Discovering ancient civilizations

The remains of ancient civilizations in Jordan are a window into human history and its rich civilizations, such as the Nabataeans, Romans, and Ottomans. Exploring these sites allows visitors to learn about ancient architecture, customs and traditions, and cultural development throughout the ages. These relics hold significant scientific and educational value, helping us understand the evolution of human societies and serving as a source of national pride and sustainable tourism, while preserving our heritage for future generations.

New tourism activities

Wadi Rum is one of Jordan's most beautiful natural sites, offering a unique experience that combines adventure and stunning nature. Visitors can take part in four-wheel-drive safaris, camp under the stars, climb and walk among the sand dunes, and enjoy camel riding and learning about authentic Bedouin culture
